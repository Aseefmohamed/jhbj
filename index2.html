    Wall Saw

    A wall saw is a tool used to cut through walls or other solid structures, like concrete or brick. It has a circular blade that spins rapidly and is mounted on a track, allowing it to make straight and precise cuts. It's commonly used in construction and renovation projects to create openings for doors, windows, or ductwork.
