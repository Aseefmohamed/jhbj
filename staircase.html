STAIRCASE CUTTING

About Staircase Cutting

Drill multiple holes near the edge of the step to form a break-away section. Use a hammer drill with a chisel bit to create weak points in the concrete. These sections can then be broken off with an 8-pound sledgehammer; several swings will be required to remove the chunks. For larger concrete demolition tasks, jackhammers are ideal. There are four types of jackhammers: rotary, demolition, combination, and breaker, each designed for specific applications.
